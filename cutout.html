Migration Timeline

  • 2016
    Decided to switch to Hyku (Samvera) from ContentDM

    Reasons to switch: support for open standards (Linked Data with Fedora 4, IIIF), more flexibility and control over how we organize and present our objects and collections.

  • 2016-2018
    Planning, Software Development, Metadata

    Installed Hyku and created a software tool for exporting metadata out of ContentDM. Metadata needed to be normalized and converted to URIs where possible.

  • 2018
    First collection uploaded, ongoing software development & metadata work

    ContentDM held 48 collections containing 8,564 individual digital works that needed to be migrated.
    We had to balance migration priorities with new collections that were being created and uploaded into Vault directly.

  • 2019
    13 collections and 2302 works uploaded to Vault
